PERSONAL TRAINING TERMS & CONDITIONS

Training Sessions & Responsibilities

  • Each session is designed to help you reach your fitness goals safely and effectively.
  • Clients must arrive on time and be prepared for their sessions. Late arrivals will result in a shortened session with no time extension.
  • The trainer is not responsible for lack of progress due to inconsistency or non-adherence to the program.

Payment & Refund Policy

  • A ₱1,000 reservation fee is required to secure your personal training slot.
    • This fee is non-refundable, whether or not the client continues with the training.
    • The amount will be deducted from the total package payment once the program begins.
  • Clients may choose from the following payment options:
    • Full Payment – Pay the entire amount upfront before starting the program.
    • Installments – Pay in agreed-upon installments; the first payment is required before the program begins. Sessions will only continue as long as payments are made on time.
    • Per Session Payment (if applicable) – Payment must be made before or immediately after each session.
  • All payments are non-refundable once the training package has started.
  • Sessions cannot be transferred to another person without prior approval.

Session Validity, Expiration & Membership Freeze

All training packages come with a specific session validity period, which will be communicated to the client upon enrollment.

Session Expiration

If the session validity period expires while there are still remaining unused sessions, the following will apply:

  • Clients will be required to pay a ₱1,500 extension/holding fee per week starting from the date of expiration until all remaining sessions are fully consumed.
  • This weekly fee allows the client to retain and continue using their remaining sessions beyond the original validity period.
  • If the client chooses not to pay the weekly extension fee, all remaining unused sessions will be automatically forfeited.
  • No refunds or transfers will be provided for forfeited sessions.

It is the client's responsibility to schedule and complete all sessions within the agreed validity period.

Membership Freeze Policy

In cases of special occasions, emergencies, medical situations, or out-of-town travel, clients may request a temporary membership freeze, subject to the following conditions:

  • The freeze request must be communicated in advance or as soon as reasonably possible.
  • Once approved, the session validity period will be paused for the duration of the freeze and will not be deducted during that time.
  • Freeze duration and approval are subject to the trainer's discretion and may require supporting documentation in certain cases.
  • Sessions will automatically resume once the freeze period ends.
  • Any sessions not frozen properly or not communicated will continue to count toward the original validity period.

Cancellation & Rescheduling Policy

  • Clients cannot cancel their training package once enrolled. The full program must be completed as agreed upon.
  • Rescheduling must be done at least 24 hours in advance. Failure to provide proper notice will result in the session being automatically forfeited.
  • If the client cancels or fails to attend after the coach has already arrived at the gym or agreed training venue, the session will be automatically considered used and deducted from the remaining sessions, as the trainer has already allocated time and resources for the appointment.
  • Repeated cancellations, late cancellations, or no-shows may result in termination of training with no refund and no carry-over of sessions.
  • In special circumstances (e.g., medical emergencies or severe injuries), exceptions may be considered at the trainer's discretion, and proper documentation may be required.

Trainer-Initiated Cancellations

If the trainer needs to cancel or reschedule a confirmed session, the following will apply:

  • The client's session validity will be extended by one (1) calendar day for each session canceled by the trainer.
  • This extension applies only to validity dates and does not add extra sessions.
  • Trainer-initiated cancellations do not affect the client's remaining session count.

Commitment & Accountability

  • Clients are expected to train consistently and put in the necessary effort to see progress.
  • Any health concerns or injuries must be reported immediately so modifications can be made.
  • The trainer will provide guidance, but results depend on the client's discipline, effort, and lifestyle choices.

Privacy & Confidentiality

  • All client information and progress will be kept confidential.
  • Clients may not record or distribute training sessions without permission.
  • The trainer may use anonymous progress updates for marketing purposes with client consent.
