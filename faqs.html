NUTRITION COACHING FAQ

Common questions about our personalized nutrition coaching approach

1. Is this a meal plan or actual nutrition coaching?

This is nutrition coaching, not just a meal plan. Coach EJ focuses on sustainable eating habits, proper portioning, food flexibility, and real-life adherence — not crash diets or overly restrictive plans.

Plans are adjusted based on your lifestyle, training schedule, food preferences, and progress over time.

2. Do I need to count calories or macros?

Not always. Depending on your goals and experience level, Coach EJ may use:

  • Portion-based guidance
  • Intuitive eating frameworks
  • Simplified macro awareness
  • Or structured macro targets (if needed)

The goal is long-term consistency, not burnout from tracking.

3. Can nutrition coaching work even if I eat out or have a busy schedule?

Yes — and this is exactly what the coaching is designed for. Coach EJ helps you:

  • Make better food choices when eating out
  • Build flexible meal options
  • Adjust nutrition around work, travel, and social events

You don't need a "perfect" routine to make progress.

4. Is nutrition coaching only for fat loss?

No. Nutrition coaching supports multiple goals, including:

  • Fat loss
  • Muscle building
  • Body recomposition
  • Performance improvement
  • General health and energy levels

Your plan is aligned with your training program and lifestyle, not a one-size-fits-all approach.

5. Will my nutrition plan change over time?

Yes. Nutrition coaching is progressive, just like training. Coach EJ regularly adjusts your plan based on:

  • Body changes
  • Training intensity
  • Lifestyle shifts
  • Plateaus or adherence challenges

This ensures your nutrition evolves as your body and goals evolve.

6. Do you accommodate food preferences or restrictions?

Absolutely. Plans are customized based on:

  • Food preferences and dislikes
  • Cultural food choices
  • Dietary restrictions or sensitivities

The goal is to create a plan you can actually follow, not suffer through.

7. Is nutrition coaching suitable for beginners?

Yes. Coach EJ works with beginners and experienced trainees alike. Beginners are guided step-by-step with:

  • Simple food structures
  • Clear expectations
  • Easy-to-follow adjustments

No prior nutrition knowledge is required.

PERSONAL TRAINING PACKAGES FAQ

Questions about our training packages, rates, and policies

1. How do your training rates work?

We offer area-based pricing depending on your location. This helps us account for travel time, scheduling, and service quality. You can view our rates by city on the Pricing page.

2. Why do rates differ by city?

Our rates are tailored based on travel distance, traffic patterns, and demand in each area. This ensures we can provide consistent quality and reliability in every location we serve.

3. Do you offer FREE sessions?

Yes — in selected areas, some packages include bonus sessions. In other areas, we offer transparent all-in pricing that doesn't rely on "free" sessions but still delivers great value. This varies by city based on local demand and client expectations.

4. What areas do you currently serve?

We currently serve:

Parañaque

Las Piñas

Alabang

Makati

Taguig

BGC

If you're outside these zones, you may still be served at applicable neighboring area rates.

5. What if I'm outside the listed areas?

We may still be able to train you! Some nearby locations are served under adjacent city rates. Just send your address and we'll let you know which rate applies.

6. Do sessions expire?

Yes — all session packages have a validity period. Unused sessions expire after that period and are non-transferable. This keeps scheduling fair for all clients.

7. Can I transfer or pause my sessions?

Sessions cannot be transferred or paused unless otherwise arranged prior to booking. This ensures schedule integrity and fairness for all clients.

8. Do you offer nutrition or program updates?

Yes — based on your package, we provide complimentary assessments and program updates to help you stay on track.

PERSONAL TRAINING FAQ'S

Common questions about how personal training works with EP Fitness

How does personal training work?

Personal training with EP Fitness is a structured, one-on-one coaching service designed around your goals, schedule, and current fitness level.

Here's how it works:

  • 1
    Initial Assessment

    We start with a movement, lifestyle, and goal assessment to understand your needs, limitations, and training history.

  • 2
    Customized Training Program

    Your program is tailored specifically for you — whether your goal is fat loss, muscle building, body recomposition, or overall fitness. Training evolves as you progress.

  • 3
    Coached Training Sessions

    Sessions are conducted one-on-one, with real-time coaching, form correction, and progression adjustments during every workout.

  • 4
    Ongoing Progression & Support

    Programs are updated based on performance, recovery, and results to ensure continuous progress without burnout or injury.

Where do training sessions take place?

Training sessions can be done at:

  • Your home
  • Your condo or subdivision gym
  • Approved training spaces (by arrangement)

This is a mobile personal training service, designed for convenience and consistency.

How long is each training session?

Each session typically lasts 60 minutes, including:

  • Warm-up and mobility work
  • Main workout
  • Cool-down or recovery guidance

Do I need my own equipment?

Not necessarily. Your coach can bring essential equipment depending on your setup. If you have access to a home or condo gym, programs will be designed around what's available.

How often should I train per week?

Most clients train 2–4 times per week, depending on:

  • Goals
  • Schedule
  • Recovery capacity

Your coach will recommend a frequency that fits your lifestyle and supports sustainable progress.

Is personal training suitable for beginners?

Yes. Personal training is ideal for beginners. Coaching includes:

  • Proper exercise instruction
  • Safe progressions
  • Clear guidance at every step

No prior gym experience is required.

Can personal training be combined with nutrition coaching?

Yes. Personal training can be combined with nutrition coaching for better results. Training and nutrition work together to:

  • Improve consistency
  • Support recovery
  • Accelerate body composition changes
